Our Coffee Journey

Founded in 2019, Brew Bangkok was born from a passion for bringing together traditional Thai coffee culture with modern brewing techniques. Our founder, Somchai Wattana, spent years studying coffee cultivation in Thailand's northern regions before opening our doors.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers in Chiang Rai and Chiang Mai, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. Each cup we serve celebrates Thailand's rich coffee heritage while embracing innovation.
