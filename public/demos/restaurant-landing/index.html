Our Story

Golden Lotus was founded in 2010 by Chef Somchai Pattana, whose culinary journey began in the vibrant street markets of Bangkok and led through the kitchens of five-star hotels across Southeast Asia.

Our philosophy is simple: to create an authentic Thai dining experience that honors traditional recipes while embracing modern culinary techniques. Each dish tells a story of Thailand's rich culinary heritage, using only the finest ingredients sourced both locally and directly from Thailand.

At Golden Lotus, we believe that dining is not just about food—it's about creating memorable experiences. Our elegant setting, attentive service, and exquisite cuisine come together to transport you to Thailand, if only for an evening.
